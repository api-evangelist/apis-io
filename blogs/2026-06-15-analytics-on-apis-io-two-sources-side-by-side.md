The APIs.io network added a new apis.io/analytics page in the More dropdown across every site that publishes its own web traffic in the open every week. It shows two independent sources side by side - Cloudflare at the edge and Google Analytics 4 in the browser - rather than picking one official number, appending a snapshot every Monday; for the week ending June 10, 2026 the two sources reported differing figures, and the gap between them is the point.
