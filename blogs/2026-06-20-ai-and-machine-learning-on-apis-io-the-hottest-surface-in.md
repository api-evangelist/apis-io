AI & Machine Learning is the largest single vertical in the APIs.io catalog at 650 providers across 3,079 APIs, and the one where the surface a vendor advertised six months ago is already stale. This is the first of six posts walking the hottest industries in the catalog, sorting the AI-infrastructure layer into roughly four functional bands - the fourth of which barely existed a year ago and is now the band everything else is reorganizing around.
