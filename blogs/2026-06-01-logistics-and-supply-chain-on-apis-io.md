Logistics and supply chain is one of the deepest verticals on APIs.io and one of the least talked about. It is also one of the most agent-relevant, as most of the work in this category involves multi-step coordination across carriers, customs, warehouses, and visibility platforms — exactly the shape autonomous agents are built for.
